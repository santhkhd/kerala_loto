How to claim the Winning Amount for Vishu Bumper (BR-109)

01

Initial Verification

Firstly, verify your ticket number by comparing it with the official announcement on the Directorate of State Lotteries website, statelottery.kerala.gov.in. If your ticket number matches the announced winning number, congratulations, you've won the ticket.

02

Ticket Submission

To claim your prize, follow the specified process provided by the authorities. Kindly visit the Department Office in Thiruvananthapuram and submit your ticket in impeccable condition. Ensure that your Ticket remains undamaged throughout the submission process.

Payment Channels

"Prize payments will be released only after verifying the authenticity of the winning lottery ticket at the designated Lottery Offices."

Alternative Claiming Path:

Winners can also claim their prize amount through any Nationalised Bank, Scheduled Bank, or State/District Co-operative Bank. Submit the original prize-winning ticket along with all required documents. The bank will forward the claim to the Director of State Lotteries, Kerala.

Verification Process

Bring along a valid ID proof such as Aadhar Card or Voter ID. Submission must be completed within 30 days from the announcement.

Prizes Below ₹5,000
